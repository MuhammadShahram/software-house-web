Internship Program

Internship Program by WeConnect

Agreement with Software House and Student

Note: Internships are totally free for some levels. See details below.

  1. Expert Level: This level is free. Candidates work Monday to Thursday, 3 hours daily,
    and the internship concludes with a job offer and a certificate after completing 3 months.

  2. Intermediate Level: Students at this level pay an initial fee of 5k.
    They receive guidance and work Monday to Thursday.
    After 3 months, they will receive a certificate. For the second month and onward,
    fee will be determined based on their performance.

  3. Beginner Level: Students at this level pay a 10k fee for the first month.
    For the subsequent months, the fee will depend on performance.

  4. Students can send their CVs to our WhatsApp contact number,
    and interviews will be conducted weekly.
